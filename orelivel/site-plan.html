Overview

Purpose

Think about the last lodging that you visit. It was easy to find? It has everything you need? Do you wait for an answer for more than an hour even a day to check available dates and make a reservation? I am a frontend developer and I will make your experience looking for an ideal place pleasant, quickly and easily. Through this website you can find a list od destinations located on beaches and mountains, where you can see available dates, check prices and see what each lodging is equipped with. You can do all of this from the comfort of your home or wherever you are, finding an ideal place has never been so easy.

Audience

Parents, workers, friends and tourists will be our target audience. People who want to enjoy a nice, safe and well-equipped lodging, where they can relax and disconnect from daily tasks and duties, and people who want to have an unforgettable experience and enjoy nature. Our typography, colors, and content will let them connect with us at a glance, by choosing color theory.

Content

Home page

Ready for some vacations?

Nowadays, people are looking for a place to relax and enjoy nature, but they don't have time to look for a place to stay. They want to find a place where they can disconnect from their daily tasks and duties, and enjoy nature. Here is the place where you can find a place to stay, where they can relax and enjoy nature.

Images for the Home page


Kind of Places

You want to enjoy an exquisite climate and a spectacular view of the Pacific Ocean, see magical sunsets, disconnect for a moment from the city, and enjoy a lodging equipped with everything you need. Here you can find different lodgings on beaches all over El Salvador, where we provide you the basic information such as time from the capital, prices, photos of each place, disponibility dates, if you can find a supermarket or a gas station near, and the equipment with what it counts.

On the other side, we have a catalog of cottages located in mountains, hills, lakes, and near the city with amazing weather; some of them have swimming pools, barbecues, camping area, and more. You can see here the equipment of each cottage, the distance from the capital, the number of people permitted, parking, and nearby establishments and restaurants. A perfect place to spend some time with your family and peers.

FAQS

Are you one of those people who can't leave their pets when they go out, well, that's not a problem not to go and enjoy a beautiful day in one of the lodgings, some of them are able to receive pets (may have an additional cost) so you can enjoy next to the ones you love the most.

Most accommodations have parking space for more than one car within their facilities, which helps you not to worry about something happening to your vehicle.
