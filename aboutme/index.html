Vale

Hi everyone, my name is Valeria Esquivel my relatives call me Vale. I was born in El Salvador, a land with the most amazing beaches, in 1999. I live with my parents, my grandmother and my sisters, we have a cat and dog which fill our home with joy. I have been a member of the church my whole life, and thanks to that I grew up enjoying the different organizations such as Primary and Young Women, I loved participating in Seminary. Since I was 18 years old I have had calls to work with young people and children.

Currently, I work as a programmer in a local company in my country, and I really love going out with my colleagues to visit places in my country. One of my hobbies is listening to music, for almost every activity I do I have to be listening to music.

I'm very excited for this new adventure, and to meet all of you, thank you for reading my page.
